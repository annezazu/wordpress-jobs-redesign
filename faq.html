Frequently Asked Questions

Head over to the Post a Job page and fill out the submission form with your job details. Once submitted, our team will review the listing and publish it within one to two business days. You'll receive a confirmation email with a link to manage your listing.

Posting a job on the WordPress Jobs board is completely free. We believe in keeping the WordPress ecosystem open and accessible, so there are no fees or premium tiers. All listings receive equal visibility on the board.

Job listings remain active on the board for 21 days from the date they are published. After that period, they are automatically archived. If you haven't filled the position yet, you're welcome to resubmit the listing for another 21-day cycle.

Yes, you can edit or remove your listing at any time using the management link sent to your email when the job was submitted. If you've lost that email, contact our jobs team and we'll help you make changes or take the listing down promptly.

This board is specifically for WordPress-related positions, including development, design, support, content writing, community management, and other roles that primarily involve working with WordPress. Listings that are not directly related to the WordPress ecosystem will be declined during review.

You can reach the WordPress Jobs team by emailing jobs@wordpress.net. We typically respond within two business days. For general WordPress support questions, please visit the WordPress Support Forums instead.
